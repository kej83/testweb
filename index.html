Biography

A voice shaped by
tradition & discovery

"Her playing grows from sincerity and curiosity — blending depth of feeling with clarity of expression and a strong personal voice."

Milkica Radovanovic stands among the most compelling concert organists and pianists of her generation. Performing across Europe's premier venues — from Westminster Abbey and Coventry Cathedral to Oslo's Nidarosdomen — she brings passion, precision, and authenticity to every concert.

A prizewinner at the International Bach Competition Leipzig (2018) and finalist at the Nordic Organ Competition (2021), Milkica holds Master's degrees in both Organ and Piano Performance from the University of Stavanger, earned with distinction. She has been honoured with a Royal Distinction from HRH Crown Prince Alexander II for outstanding contributions to the arts.

International Tour · 2024–2026

Trinity of Sound:
Bach, Dupré & Nystedt

A landmark concert series across the United Kingdom, Germany, and Norway celebrating the connection between tradition, modernism, and spirituality — aligning with the major anniversaries of Bach 340 and Nystedt 110. Milkica has become one of the foremost international advocates of Norwegian composers such as Egil Hovland and Knut Nystedt.

Education & Outreach

Masterclasses &
Academic Teaching

Milkica brings the same depth of insight to pedagogy as she does to performance. Guest lecturer at Luleå University of Technology and the School of Music in Piteå, she inspires the next generation of musicians with artistry and pedagogical clarity. She holds a teaching position in Ullensaker Kommune, Norway.
